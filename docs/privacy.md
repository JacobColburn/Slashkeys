# SlashKeys Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 6, 2026_

SlashKeys is a local-first text expander. We designed it so that we never see your data.

## What SlashKeys stores

- **Your snippets** (triggers, descriptions, and expansion text) are stored locally in
  your browser using Chrome's extension storage. They never leave your device.
- **Your settings** (on/off state, disabled sites) are stored the same way.

## What SlashKeys reads

- To detect when you type a `\trigger`, the extension observes text you type into
  editable fields on web pages. This matching happens **entirely on your device**.
  Nothing you type is transmitted, logged, or stored beyond the snippet matching that
  makes expansion work. Password fields are always ignored.
- The `{clipboard}` placeholder (Pro) reads your clipboard **only** at the moment a
  snippet containing it expands, and only if you have granted the optional clipboard
  permission.

## What SlashKeys transmits

- **Nothing**, with one exception: if you purchase SlashKeys Pro, the extension
  contacts [ExtensionPay](https://extensionpay.com) (our payment processor, built on
  Stripe) to verify your license. No snippet content or typed text is ever included.

## What we don't do

- No analytics, no tracking, no ads, no accounts.
- We do not sell, share, or collect personal data. We couldn't — it never reaches us.
